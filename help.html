Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I use Assure?

It’s simple! Our home screen displays the recommended stocks for the day. From there, choose which stocks interest you (perhaps look at our reasons for recommendations), login to your broker and buy the stock close to the opening price. Once the stock price has risen to your desired price, you can sell it. For those who ask “really?”, you can go to our ‘Past Picks’ section and judge the effectiveness of our algorithm yourselves! For those who then say “show me more!”, go right on to our ‘Simulate’ section and try out how profitable the stocks are yourselves!

2.Does Assure allow me to trade on its platform?

No, we do not allow live trading on our platform. However, we have a simple simulation segment that allows the user to see how effective our past recommendations are.

3. Can I change the tags for the stocks Assure recommends?

Absolutely! The tags can be changed to suit the user's choice of stocks to invest. Nevertheless, without customisation, the tags would automatically be set to the most popular types of stocks!

4. Am I guaranteed to make money in every stock that is recommended?

Unfortunately, there is no sure way to make sure that every stock that is recommended will be profitable. However, based on backtesting, we strive to beat the odds such that in every stock that we recommend, there is a high probability that it would be profitable. In the long run, investors will be sure to grow their wealth.

5. Can I delete my Assure account?

You can unsubscribe from Assure by going into your profile and selecting “unsubscribe”. There are no cancellation fees.

6. How much money do I need to start investing?

With the wide range of stocks Assure recommends, you will easily be able to find a stock that fits your budget. Stock prices can range from a few dollars to hundreds of dollars. There really is no minimum amount to start investing!

7. Can I see the daily stock picks without an Assure account?

No, the daily stock picks are only available if you have an Assure account. Nevertheless, you can open a 30 day trial account with us to see our daily stock picks within those 30 days!
